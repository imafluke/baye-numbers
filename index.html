Pricing & tiers

Different levels — by how much data you see, and how many Baye tokens you burn on the agent.

We meter on Baye tokens, not flat requests. Every question burns real AI tokens — light asks are cheap, deep briefs cost more. We charge a 3× markup on our cost (slider in ROI). If a request costs us $1 in AI tokens, the user pays that multiple from their balance. Wallet loads: 1,000 Baye tokens / $1. Subscriptions: 1,200 / $1 (+20% pool each month).
