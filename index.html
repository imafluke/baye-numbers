Pay-as-you-go · the wallet Big feature

For people who just want the agent — no subscription.

Stay on Free, load a balance, and use the Baye agent whenever — and the balance never expires. Auto-reload keeps it topped up so you never stall. Priced as a premium per request, so a subscription is the better deal for regulars — but the wallet is perfect for the occasional bettor.

Same $20 — two different deals

All-Star subscription80 req + full data · resets monthly
Wallet (agent only)33 requests · never expire

The subscription gives more requests and the full data tools — but they reset every month and don't roll over. The wallet gives fewer requests at a higher price, but they never expire. Load $20 once and use it whenever — ideal for the occasional bettor. Regulars subscribe; everyone else tops up.
